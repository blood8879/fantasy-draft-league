# Rating Rubric

Ratings are original game-balance interpretations, not official rankings and not derived from other football games or proprietary databases.

## Axes

- `Scoring`: finishing, goal threat, penalty-area value.
- `Creation`: final pass, chance creation, attacking imagination.
- `Progression`: carrying, dribbling, line-breaking actions.
- `Control`: tempo, pass security, pressure resistance.
- `Defense`: defensive work, tackling, interceptions, tracking.
- `Physical`: strength, aerial value, duel capacity.
- `Mobility`: pace, agility, work rate, transition coverage.
- `Mental`: major-match influence, consistency, leadership, tactical clarity.

## Grades

- `S`: era-defining elite for the card's year context.
- `A`: world-class strength.
- `B`: strong high-level contributor.
- `C`: usable but limited.
- `D`: clear weakness at this game's challenge level.

## Data Policy

- Use public factual sources for biographical and career context.
- Store source references on `Player` records.
- Do not ingest Football Manager, EA FC, eFootball, Transfermarkt market values, or proprietary ratings.
- Add a rationale for every card with at least one `S` grade.
